About Me

My Introduction

Hi! I'm Sian Louise Hazelton, and I'm an aspiring developer based in London.

My background is in English - I have an English Literature and Creative Writing BA + English Literature MA. I also worked as an Assistant Language teacher in Japan.
I am a creative person and have many different interests, including sewing, poetry and video games.

After returning from teaching abroad I decided to pursue coding, as I was always interested in it but never took the plunge. This led me to partaking in a 3-month Software Development Bootcamp to improve my skills, and aim for a new career that combines my creative and technical skills!
